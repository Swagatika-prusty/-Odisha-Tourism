Besides the heritage monuments and the shopping markets, the water parks in Bhubaneswar are equally popular among the localities. Boasting of a plethora of water rides, roller coasters, multiple swimming pools etc, these water parks in Bhubaneswar are slowly gaining popularity for a perfect day out among families, friends and couples alike.

Summers in the city are pretty harsh with the scorching sun making any outdoor activity impossible, it is during those days that you can head out to the pools to relax and rewind soaked in the cool and calm waters of these gorgeous water parks in Bhubaneswar.

This is a great spot to spend an entire day with the complete family because it has lots of variety. Even though it isn't officially a water park in BBSR (short for Bhubaneswar), you may still embrace a few fantastic water rides here. Aside from the coasters, the park features a nicely designed artificial lake where you may go paddle boating. There's also a mini-golf course where both skilled and novice players may have a good time. You can always try your luck at golf here, whether you know how to play or not!
