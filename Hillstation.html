TAPTAPANI


Address :Address :ganjam P.O.-ganjam, Dist.- ganjam, A Hot Water Spring, Orissa, India
Hours :9AM-6PM

Management : Government of India

Entry point to Taptapani is Berhampur. This southern town of Orissa is located at a distance of 170 KM from Bhubaneswar. This town is well connected by road through NH 5 and Berhampur railway station is in the main train line connecting Howrah Railway station at Kolkata and Chennai. All trains stops at Berhampur ( Station Code : BAM ) railway station.

If you are travelling from South that is from Visakhapatnam then drive on NH 5 upto Itchapuram . Enter into Itchapuram town and cross bus stand. Before joining again to NH5 take left turn and enter Digapandi road.

Minimum two nights are recommended for Taptapani. If you are reaching in afternoon at Taptapani then take rest in resort. Start your day trip after breakfast. You will return in evening and night you can take rest at Taptapani resort.


MAHENDRAGIRI


Address :mahendragiri hillstation,Gajapati, Orissa, India.
Hours :9AM-6PM
Management :Ministry of Environment and Forests, Government of India

Mahendragiri is associated with the Ramayana as Mahendra Parvata (mountain). It is a 'Kula Parvata'along with Malaya, Sahyadri, Parijatra, Shuktiman, Vindhya and Malyavaan.
Mahendragiri is an undeveloped but beautiful Hill Station in Odisha. The roads are not so good, so go prepared accordingly. There is no accommodation here. Not a family tour destination. My friends and I camped on top of the mountain.
Mahendragiri is a hill station located in the Paralakhemundi subdivision of the district of Gajapati in Orissa. A part of the Eastern Ghats, the mountain peak is worth a visit for the views if not for the sights, sounds and culture on offer.
